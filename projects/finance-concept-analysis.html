Finance Concept Analysis

A learning and simulation workspace for quantitative finance concepts and personal-finance decision-making.

Problem

Many finance concepts are unintuitive; small simulations and structured notes help validate intuition and communicate decision biases.

Context and constraints

  • Organizes simulators, papers, and reports
  • Includes gambler’s fallacy and lifecycle investment learning components
  • Constraints: educational framing; avoid invented performance metrics

Approach

  • Provide small simulators for intuition-building
  • Organize reading notes and summaries
  • Document experiments so others can reproduce them

Architecture

flowchart LR
  A[Concept / hypothesis] --> B[Simulator]
  B --> C[Run experiments]
  C --> D[Visualize / summarize]
  D --> E[Notes & interpretations]

Implementation highlights

  • Clear structure for simulations and references
  • Reproducible experiments with documented parameters
  • Teaching-oriented framing for explaining finance heuristics

Results and impact

Educational project demonstrating finance concepts through reproducible simulations and documentation (no numeric performance claims).

Tech stack

Python, simulation, documentation.
